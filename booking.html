Booking

Minimum is $200

I book all my appointments through DMs
When sending me a message please include the following:
  • Any preferred pronouns
  • Description of what you’re looking to get -- the more details the better
  • Area you would like tattooed (photos help a ton)
  • Tattoo size in inches (small,medium,large is not an acceptable answer)
  • Color or B&W
  • 2-3 reference photos if you have any; please note of any specific things you like or don't like (please keep in mind I will not copy another artists work, I will adapt it into my own style of tattooing)
$80-$150 booking fee required (zelle or venmo) (please see policies for more info) Please expect 1-3 days for a response. If I don't get back to you within that time frame, please feel free to send me another message.

Subjects I enjoy tattooing:

Anything colorful, bold, nature related, neo trad portraits / ladyhead, and anime inspired styles of tattooing! Im open to most ideas.

Things I will not do:

  • I will not tattoo your face/head/neck/hands or fingers if you don’t already have multiple tattoos
  • Hate symbols
  • Cultural tattoos -- I will not tattoo Asian style dragons (there’s plenty of different dragons to choose from, let’s make it a custom one!), Polynesian tribal tattoos, or Native American symbolism (I might make exceptions if I trust the client)
  • I will not tattoo your partner's name on you unless it is a memorial tattoo
  • Armbands/wraparounds -- wrap around dragons/tribal arm bands/wrap around snakes etc. Vines are fine if they wrap around once.
  • Realism portraits of family members -- I'm open to doing portraits of models or celebrities, but I will not tattoo a realism portrait of a family member
