Amed
Artist, est. 2022
Long Beach, CA
I’m a 24 year old Latino/queer artist born in Mexico and raised in Long Beach. I have been tattooing since 2022 and am still a growing artist.
I am very versatile -- being able to take on projects in many different styles.
The style I mainly specialize in is bold neo traditional inspired color work and black and grey illustrative work -- mixing both dotted/stippled shading and smooth shading. I also really love doing anything anime/cartoon inspired!

Some of my hobbies include video games (im a sweat at fortnite), rollerskating, plant collecting, huge pokemon fan, blind box collecting, and aquarium hobbyist. I love watching animes and I also love going out and dancing.
