My name's Amed! I’m a 24 year old Latino/queer artist born in Mexico and raised in Long Beach. I’ve been tattooing for about 2ish years now( hitting 3 years in October) and still a growing artist. I'm a very versatile artist -- being able to take on projects in many different styles. The style I mainly specialize in is bold neo traditional inspired color work and black & grey illustrative work -- mixing both dotted/stippled shading and smooth shading :) I also really love doing anything anime/cartoon inspired!

Some of my hobbies include video games (im a sweat at fortnite), rollerskating, plant collecting, huge pokemon fan, blind box collecting, and aquarium hobbyist. I love watching animes and I also love going out and dancing.
