My Policies

  • I require an $80-$150 non-refundable booking fee from each person and for each appointment
  • The booking fee is valid for one reschedule if it is done within 72 hours prior to the appointment time and date
  • If you do need to reschedule, please do so as soon as possible to avoid any price changes (the more experience I gain as an artist the higher my prices will increase)
  • Failure to reschedule after 3 months without any kind of update will forfeit a deposit. Keep me updated and I’ll hold on to it longer; I’m a reasonable guy just communicate with me
  • Booking fee is non-transferable to other people
  • The booking fee will go towards the final total of the tattoo -- the rest of the remaining payment will be due the day of your appointment
  • Last minute cancellations will forfeit your booking fee
  • Last minute cancellations for a touch up will result in having to pay for the touch up when you reschedule
  • Please let me know if you are not feeling well, sunburned, or on any kind of antibiotics/medication/fighting off any infections so we can reschedule
  • Must be 18 years or older
  • Design will be shown the day of the appointment
  • Booking an appointment & paying the booking fee means you agree and understand my policies
